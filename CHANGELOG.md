### Added

### Current Behavior

- Provides per-monitor dim overlays with configurable opacity, tint, theme, and animations.
- Includes source, installer, uninstaller, and release build workflows.
- Stores user settings under `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Screen_Dimmer`.
